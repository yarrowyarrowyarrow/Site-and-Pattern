# Learning Journal

Free-form, yours, committed along with your progress ticks. Many lessons
ask you to "journal" something — this is where it goes. The habit that
makes it work: after AI (or a doc, or a test) explains something to you,
close it and re-explain here in your own words. If you can't, you haven't
got it yet — and now you know.

Suggested entry shape (bend it freely):

```
## 2026-07-21 — L0.1
What I did:
What clicked:
What's still foggy:
```

No test reads this file. Nothing here can be wrong.
